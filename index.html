02 · Foundation

Brand Story

Veridoshé is a luxury zero-proof spirit crafted to deliver the full cocktail experience without compromise. Our flagship expressions — GINesis and GINcognito — deliver seamless mixability across gin & tonics, margaritas, martinis, and more. As today's Millennial & Gen Z consumers drink less, they're seeking premium, authentic, and health-conscious alternatives. Veridoshé isn't just a spirit — it's a movement redefining nightlife, culture, and conscious choice.

08 · Visual System

Visual Texture

No decorative gradients. Textures must be subtle, tactile, and purposeful.

✓ Approved Textures
  • Brushed metal and matte surfaces
  • Polished dark stone / slate
  • Controlled dark glass reflections
  • Very subtle noise grain (max 5% opacity)
  • Solid dark backgrounds with one light source
✕ Avoid
  • Decorative colour gradients in UI
  • Busy patterns or loud backgrounds
  • Plastic or reflective cheap surfaces
  • Neon bar aesthetic clichés
  • Heavy noise or over-processed overlays

15 · Guidelines

Do's & Don'ts

✓ Always
  • Always VERIDOSHÉ — É accent is mandatory
  • Tagline period: "TRUTH TASTES BETTER." — always present
  • Wide tracking (.26em) on tagline in all lockups
  • Dark-first for all digital output
  • Gold (#C8A97E) as dominant accent on every layout
  • Logo as .svg only — never recreate from type
  • Juniper colour in GINesis context only
✕ Never
  • VERIDOSHE without the É accent
  • Pure white #FFFFFF — use Ivory #FDF6EA
  • Pure black #000000 — use Obsidian #0D0C08
  • New hue colours outside the defined 9
  • Drop shadows, outlines, or effects on the logo
  • Inter, Roboto, Arial, or system fonts
  • Decorative gradients in UI elements
