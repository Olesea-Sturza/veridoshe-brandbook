02 · Foundation

Brand Story

Veridoshé is a luxury zero-proof spirit crafted to deliver the full cocktail experience without compromise. Our flagship expressions — GINesis and GINcognito — deliver seamless mixability across gin & tonics, margaritas, martinis, and more. As today's Millennial & Gen Z consumers drink less, they're seeking premium, authentic, and health-conscious alternatives. Veridoshé isn't just a spirit — it's a movement redefining nightlife, culture, and conscious choice.

08 · Visual System

Visual Texture

Imagery should feel premium, editorial, and tactile. The goal: luxury-bar atmosphere, minimal composition, and controlled highlights.

✓ Approved Textures
  • Brushed metal surfaces
  • Matte stone / slate
  • Dark glass reflections
  • Subtle grain overlay (very light)
  • Soft gradients on dark backgrounds
✕ Avoid
  • Busy patterns or loud backgrounds
  • Bright colourful textures
  • Plastic or cheap surfaces
  • "Neon bar" visual clichés
  • Heavy noise or overdone overlays

15 · Guidelines

Do's & Don'ts

✓ Always
  • Always VERIDOSHÉ — never VERIDOSHE (É is mandatory)
  • Tagline period is mandatory: "TRUTH TASTES BETTER."
  • Wide tracking (.26em) on tagline in all lockups
  • Dark-first for all digital output
  • Gold (#C8A97E) as dominant accent on every layout
  • Logo always as .svg — never recreate from type
  • Cormorant Italic for editorial accents and quotes only
  • Juniper only in GINesis product contexts
✕ Never
  • Never VERIDOSHE without the É accent
  • Never pure white #FFFFFF — use Ivory #FDF6EA
  • Never pure black #000000 — use Obsidian #0D0C08
  • Never new hue colours — only opacity variants approved
  • Never drop shadows on the logo
  • Never Inter, Roboto, or Arial typefaces
  • Never Juniper outside GINesis context
  • Never mix GINesis and GINcognito in one visual
