02 · Brand

Brand Story

Veridoshé is a luxury zero-proof spirit crafted to deliver the full cocktail experience without compromise. Our flagship expressions — GINesis and Founder’s Batch — deliver seamless mixability across gin & tonics, margaritas, martinis, and more. As today’s Millennial & Gen Z consumers drink less, they’re seeking premium, authentic, and health-conscious alternatives. Veridoshé isn’t just a spirit — it’s a movement redefining nightlife, culture, and conscious choice.

03 · Visual System

Visual Texture

Imagery should feel premium, editorial, and tactile. The goal: luxury-bar atmosphere, minimal composition, and controlled highlights.

✓ Approved textures
  • Brushed metal
  • Matte stone / slate
  • Dark glass reflections
  • Subtle grain (very light)
  • Soft gradients on dark backgrounds
✕ Avoid
  • Busy patterns
  • Bright colorful textures
  • Plastic surfaces
  • Cheap “neon bar” looks
  • Overdone overlays / heavy noise

Lighting

Lighting must create a luxury editorial mood: soft directional light, controlled contrast, and reflective highlights that shape the bottle and glass.

Lighting Guideline
Key light Soft directional (30°–60°). Emphasize bottle contours and glass reflections.
Contrast High contrast shadows allowed, but keep detail in label and edges.
Background Prefer dark gradients or minimal environments. No flat bright white.
Avoid Harsh flash, flat studio light, blown highlights on glass.
Photography style: minimal compositions, premium bar mood, close product shots, negative space for type.

10 · Rules

Do's & Don'ts

✓ Do
  • Always VERIDOSHÉ — never VERIDOSHE
  • Tagline period mandatory: “TRUTH TASTES BETTER.”
  • Tagline gets wide tracking (.26em)
  • Dark-first for all digital output
  • Gold (#c8a97e) is the dominant accent on every layout
  • Logo always as SVG — never from type
  • Playfair Italic for editorial accents and quotes only
✕ Don't
  • Never VERIDOSHE without the É accent
  • Never pure white #FFFFFF — use Ivory #FDF6EA
  • Never pure black #000000 — use Obsidian #0D0C08
  • Never forest green outside GINesis
  • Never drop shadows on the logo
  • Never Inter, Roboto, or Arial
  • Never new hue colors — only opacity variants
