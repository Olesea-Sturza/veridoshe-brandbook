02 · Foundation
Brand Story

Veridoshé is a luxury zero-proof spirit crafted to deliver the full cocktail experience without compromise. Our flagship expressions — GINesis and GINcognito — deliver seamless mixability across gin & tonics, margaritas, martinis, and more. As today's Millennial & Gen Z consumers drink less, they're seeking premium, authentic, and health-conscious alternatives. Veridoshé isn't just a spirit — it's a movement redefining nightlife, culture, and conscious choice.

Tagline
Truth Tastes Better.
Brand Principles
Mission
Redefine premium cocktail culture by creating sophisticated zero-proof spirits.
Vision
A world where premium cocktails are defined by flavor and experience — not alcohol.
Values
Sophistication · Authenticity · Conscious enjoyment · Innovation · Premium quality
Tone of voice
Confident · Minimal · Intelligent · Refined · Modern. Avoid playful tone.

09 · Visual System

Visual Texture

Textures are used sparingly and must remain subtle.

Approved Textures
  • Brushed metal and matte surfaces
  • Polished dark stone / slate
  • Controlled dark glass reflections
  • Subtle film grain (≤5%)
  • Solid dark backgrounds with one light source
Avoid
  • Decorative colour gradients in UI
  • Busy patterns or loud backgrounds
  • Plastic or reflective cheap surfaces
  • Neon bar aesthetic clichés
  • Heavy noise or over-processed overlays

10 · Visual System

Photography Guidelines

Every image must reinforce the brand: premium, editorial, tactile. No stock photography clichés.

Shot Types
Hero Shot
Bottle centred, dark void background, single directional key light from 30–45°.
Lifestyle Shot
Cocktail glass + garnish in a premium bar setting. Minimal props. Space for copy.
Detail Shot
Close botanicals — juniper, herbs, citrus. Shallow DOF. Matte surfaces only.
Editorial Shot
Atmospheric, person in luxury setting. Emotion over product. Gold as accent only.
Lighting Setup
Key light30–45° from left. Soft directional. Creates bottle contour and gold reflections.
Fill lightVery subtle, preserves label detail in shadows.
BacklightOptional rim. Creates separation from background.
BackgroundDark void only. Never flat white.
Technical Specifications
Minimum resolution
3000 × 3000 px
300 DPI print · 72 DPI web
Colour profile
sRGB for digital
CMYK for print · Adobe RGB optional
Delivery format
.RAW + .JPG master
Export .webp for web delivery
Photography Do
  • Dark-first compositions with controlled highlights
  • Minimal props — bottle, glass, one garnish maximum
  • Negative space left for typography overlay
  • Premium textures: marble, slate, brushed metal
  • Mood and emotion first — product second
Photography Don't
  • Bright studio white backgrounds
  • Busy scenes with too many elements
  • Overexposed or blown highlights on glass
  • Stock photo aesthetics or generic setups
  • GINesis and GINcognito in the same image

14 · Marketing

Marketing Guidelines

Do
  • Dark-first layouts with gold as dominant accent
  • Minimal type overlays, editorial tone of voice
  • Product + ritual: glass, garnish, premium setting
  • Use approved colour pairings only
  • Use generous negative space.
Don't
  • Bright or colourful palettes outside the system
  • Overloaded text blocks or cluttered layouts
  • Cheap sticker icons, emojis, or clip art
  • Do not mix product identities within a single visual.
  • Playful or casual tone of voice

16 · Guidelines

Do's & Don'ts

Always
  • Always VERIDOSHÉ — É accent is mandatory
  • Tagline period: "TRUTH TASTES BETTER." — always present
  • Wide tracking (.26em) on tagline in all lockups
  • Dark-first for all digital output
  • Gold (#C8A97E) as dominant accent on every layout
  • Logo as .svg only — never recreate from type
  • Juniper colour in GINesis context only
Never
  • VERIDOSHE without the É accent
  • Pure white --ivory — use Ivory #FDF6EA
  • Pure black #000000 — use Obsidian #0D0C08
  • New hue colours outside the defined 9
  • Drop shadows, outlines, or effects on the logo
  • Inter, Roboto, Arial, or system fonts
  • Decorative gradients in UI elements
